Three Minutes Speech On Managerial Skills



Assalamualaikum to everyone. Hope you are doing well in this tough time.

• What is managerial skill?
• What are different kinds of managerial skills with examples?
• Which level of manager uses which skills?

So, at first, Managerial skills are a kind of skill, by using these skills a manager fulfill some specific management tasks.
Robert L Katz identifies three types of skills that are essential for a successful management process. They are-

• Technical skills
• Conceptual skills and
• Human skills

• Firstly, Technical Skills:

Technical skills are closely related to the technical or engineering dimension of management. It is most important for first-level managers.
• For example- In a Software Company the Knowledge of Unix/Linux Operating System, Java/C++/Perl Programming Language, MySQL/Oracle Database Management, XML etc. are known as technical skills.

• Secondly, Conceptual Skills:

Conceptual skills are a kind of ability of a manager for more abstract thinking. That means if a manager has proper conceptual skills, then he can easily analyze and diagnose of different problems in a company. Conceptual skills are vital for top managers, less critical for mid-level managers and not required for first-level managers.
• Example of conceptual skills are creativity, decision making, problem-solving, etc.

• At last, comes Human skill:

Human skill is a manager’s ability to work with people. I think One of the most critical management tasks is to work with people. It is needed equally for all types of managers.
• Examples of human skills are effective communication (both verbal and written), motivating others, and rising team spirit etc.
